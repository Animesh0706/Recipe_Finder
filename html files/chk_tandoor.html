Tandoori Chicken

Description

A classic Indian dish with tender chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then roasted to perfection. The vibrant red color comes from the spices used in the marinade, giving it a distinctive look and flavor.

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, and spices for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat Pan in high flame.
  3. Place chicken on a baking sheet and cook for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Baste with butter and broil for 5 minutes until charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and naan bread.
