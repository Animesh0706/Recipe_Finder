Cooking Tips

Ensuring safe food handling and healthy practices for all cook.

Kitchen Essentials & Preparation

  • Keep knives sharp – A sharp knife is safer and makes cutting easier.
  • Organize ingredients beforehand – This helps in smooth and quick cooking (mise en place).
  • Use measuring tools – Accuracy matters, especially in baking.
  • Keep your workspace clean – A tidy kitchen leads to better efficiency.
  • Read the recipe twice – Avoid mistakes by understanding the steps before starting.

Cooking Techniques & Flavor Enhancements

  • Preheat pans properly – This prevents food from sticking and ensures even cooking.
  • Use salt in layers – Season food at different stages for the best flavor.
  • Let meat rest before cutting – This keeps it juicy and prevents dryness.
  • Balance flavors – Use acidity (lemon/vinegar) to cut through richness.
  • Don't overcrowd the pan – Cooking in batches prevents steaming and ensures crispiness.

Smart Cooking & Storage Hacks

  • Store herbs properly – Keep fresh herbs in a damp paper towel inside a ziplock.
  • Revive stale bread – Sprinkle water and bake for a few minutes to refresh it.
  • Use ice cube trays for sauces – Freeze leftover sauces or broth for future use.
  • Peel garlic easily – Crush the clove slightly, and the skin comes off effortlessly.
  • Reheat food with a damp towel – This keeps leftovers from drying out in the microwave.
