Mughlai Soya Chaap

Description

Mughlai Soya Chaap is a rich and aromatic dish made from soy protein, marinated in a blend of Mughlai spices, yogurt, and herbs. Grilled to perfection, it offers a tender, smoky texture with a flavorful, creamy coating, making it a popular vegetarian alternative to traditional kebabs.

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, add soya chaap pieces, and lightly roast them until golden brown. Keep aside.
  2. In the same pan, add more oil, sauté onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Lower the flame, add turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well, then add curd and cashew paste, stirring continuously to avoid curdling.
  4. Add the roasted soya chaap pieces, mix well, and cook for 5-7 minutes on low flame. Pour in cream and sprinkle garam masala & kasuri methi for rich flavor.
  5. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot with naan or paratha.
