Chicken Chilly

Description

Chicken Chilly is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer made by tossing tender chicken pieces in a zesty blend of soy sauce, chili sauce, and a medley of spices. The dish is stir-fried with onions, bell peppers, and garlic, creating a perfect balance of crunchy and soft textures with a fiery, tangy kick.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken with cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, black pepper, ginger-garlic paste, and egg.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, sauté green chilies, onions, and capsicum for 1-2 minutes on high flame.
  3. Lower the flame, add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, salt, and sugar. Mix well.
  4. Add the fried chicken to the sauce mixture, stir well, and cook for 2-3 minutes until well-coated.
  5. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
