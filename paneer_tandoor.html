Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a blend of yogurt, spices, and herbs. Grilled to perfection, it is served with a tangy mint chutney, offering a delicious mix of smoky, spicy, and creamy flavors.

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, gram flour, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, spices (turmeric, red chili, garam masala), and salt.
  2. Cut paneer into cubes and coat them well with the marinade; let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Optionally, mix bell peppers and onions with the marinade for extra flavor.
  4. Skewer paneer and veggies, then grill on a tandoor, pan, or oven until golden brown.
  5. Garnish with chaat masala, lemon juice, and serve with mint chutney.
