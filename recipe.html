Tandoori Chicken

A classic Indian dish with tender chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then roasted to perfection. It has a rich, smoky flavor and is served with a tangy mint chutney.
The vibrant red color comes from the spices used in the marinade, giving it a distinctive look and flavor.

    Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Salt

Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a blend of yogurt, spices, and herbs. Grilled to perfection, it is served with a tangy mint chutney, offering a delicious mix of smoky, spicy, and creamy flavors.
The a vibrant golden-brown color, with a slightly charred appearance from grilling.

    Ingredients:
  • Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese)
  • Yogurt
  • Veggies
  • Lemon Juice
  • Spices
  • Salt

Mughlai Soya Chaap

Mughlai Soya Chaap is a rich and aromatic dish made from soy protein, marinated in a blend of Mughlai spices, yogurt, and herbs. Grilled to perfection, it offers a tender, smoky texture with a flavorful, creamy coating, making it a popular vegetarian alternative to traditional kebabs.

    Ingredients:
  • Soy chunks or flour
  • Marinades
  • Spices
  • Salt

Chicken Chilly

Chicken Chilly is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer made by tossing tender chicken pieces in a zesty blend of soy sauce, chili sauce, and a medley of spices. The dish is stir-fried with onions, bell peppers, and garlic, creating a perfect balance of crunchy and soft textures with a fiery, tangy kick.
Often garnished with fresh cilantro, it's a mouthwatering combination of heat, sweetness, and savory goodness, making it a favorite in both casual and fine dining settings.

    Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Marinades
  • Veggies
  • Spices
  • Sauces
  • Salt
